Lewis Family Pets


Our family has been blessed with wonderful pets over the past 6 years, with our oldest daughter being their overlord/caretaker. What follows is a brief description of the Lewis Family Pets:

  • 2009 - Addie: Our first true animal we had living in our house. Part cat, part human.

  • 2012 - Mr. Myagi: We picked him from the pound in Rota, Spain. He was a quiet, shy little guy. The second we got him back to the house he showed his true colors; bright red, with horns!

  • 2013 - Izzy: The wildest of the bunch, not sure which species; possibly monkey?

  • 2013 - Precious: She truly was precious, black as night, but a heart of gold.

  • 2014 - Tigre: A beautiful cat, striped like a tiger. He also had the instinct of a tiger, and occasionally hunted Izzy.

  • 2015 - Beau: Saved by my beautiful wife; Beau was a wonderful dog. Although he had the most audible tounge; he was a sweetheart.

  • 2017 - The Barn Cats: I don't remember their names, but Addie does! Addie was their master; the head of their Clan.
